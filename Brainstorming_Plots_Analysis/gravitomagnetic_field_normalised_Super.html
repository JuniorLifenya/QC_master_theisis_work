Gravitomagnetic Field B̂g

GW propagates along x̂. In the Foldy-Wouthuysen frame, the spin couples via a Zeeman-like term. The visualised field is:

ĤFW ⊃ −(κ/4m) ĥ+/×(t,x) (p̂y² − p̂z²) σ̂x
B̂g = ∇ × [ĥ · p̂]  ≡  curl of GW–momentum coupling

h+: yy/zz sector (quadrupolar, opposite transverse signs)
h×: yz sector (quadrupolar, equal transverse signs)
